An interactive digital companion to the Glasgow Botanic Gardens Tree Trail

20 Tree Specimens

Discover detailed information about twenty carefully selected tree specimens along our trail.

Mobile Friendly

Optimized for mobile devices to enhance your on-site experience while exploring the gardens.

This comprehensive digital resource provides extended information on all twenty tree specimens featured along the Glasgow Botanic Gardens Tree Trail. Each entry includes botanical details, historical context, and interesting facts about these remarkable trees.

Whether you're visiting the gardens or exploring from home, this online companion enhances your understanding and appreciation of our diverse tree collection.
